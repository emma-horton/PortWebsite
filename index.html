About me

I am a computer science student at the University of St Andrews with a passion for learning about and experimenting with technology.

Languages

My main programming languages are Python, web-based languages (JavaScript/HTML/CSS) and SQL. I also have experience programming in C and MATLAB.

Frameworks

I have worked with a variety of ML tools such as Scikit-Learn, Pandas, XGBoost, NumPy, Matplotlib, PyTorch, Seaborn, OpenCV, NLTK; and general frameworks such as Django, Boostrap, Node.JS, D3.js.

Tools

A few of the tools that I have used on a daily basis include IDEs VSCode and PyCharm, JupyterLab, git, docker, and different OS (macOS, Ubuntu, Windows).
